Team Signup

The Chubbuck Lions Club and our Co-hosts, Idaho Central Credit Union, the Phil Meador Group, and Riverbend Communications are proud to present the 2nd annual Bill Parrish Family 3-on-3 Basketball Tournament.

We appreciate the support of the community and are thrilled that this has become an annual event. All of the information needed to register your team is found below.

  • Dates

    July 31: Optional Pool Play- Begins with team check-in from 4-5 pm, followed by group play. Games on Friday night will determine seeding for Saturday, and games will be "call your own foul".
    August 1: Double-Elimination Tournament- Team check-in from 8-9 am. Play starts at 9 and will go until each division has finished playing its championship match. If you cannot, or do not wish to play on Friday night, you can come only on Saturday, understanding that only 2 games are guaranteed. Saturday, games will be officiated.

  • Where

    Holt Arena Parking Lot

  • Cost

    $100 per team preregistered
    $125 per team sign up day-of tournament
    Teams can have up to 4 players

  • Divisions

    For younger divisions, registration is based on the grade you will be attending in the coming school year.
    • PeeWee Divison: Open up to 6th grade
    • Liam Division: 7th-8th grade
    • Keegan Division: 9th-12th grade
    • Competitive Division: Men's Open
    • Bill Division: Men's Over 30
    • Ross Division A: Women's Open up to 12th grade
    • Ross Division B: Open 18+ (After High School)

  • Proceeds will be used support awareness of Carbon Monoxide poisoning and Lions Programs in support of sight and hearing preservation and restoration.
  • Other events on saturday will include a Slam Dunk Contest, 3-point shootout, and a free throw shooting competition, according to demand.

Some details are subject to change. Please check the tournament website for updates prior to the event.
If you have any questions, or would like to make cash payment, please call Fred Parrish at (208) 406-3502, or Wilson Parrish at (208) 406-3504.
